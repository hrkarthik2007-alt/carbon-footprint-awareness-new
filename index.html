04 / TREND
How these numbers are calculated

Figures are simplified, commonly-cited emission averages meant to build intuition, not a certified carbon audit. The 190 kg/month dashed line is a rough monthly slice of the ~2.3 t CO₂e/year per-person budget often cited as 1.5°C-aligned by 2030.

  • Travel: 0.192 kg/km petrol car, 0.171 diesel, 0.103 two-wheeler, 0.105 bus, 0.041 metro/train, ~500 kg per domestic round-trip flight.
  • Energy: 0.82 kg/kWh (India grid avg) or 0.475 kg/kWh (global avg); ~42 kg CO₂e per 14.2 kg LPG cylinder.
  • Diet: ~3.3 kg/day meat-heavy, 2.5 mixed, 1.7 vegetarian, 1.5 vegan — applied across 30 days.
  • Waste: ~0.5 kg CO₂e per kg landfilled per week, reduced 40% if composted or recycled.
